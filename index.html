About us

We are a dedicated group of students from Sultan Qaboos University, passionate about leveraging technology to make learning more accessible and engaging. As part of our COMP3700 - Introduction to Web Computing course, we developed SkillMatch, a platform designed to connect learners with expert instructors in various skills, including coding, marketing, swimming, and more. Our goal is to create an interactive, user-friendly, and efficient learning environment where individuals can find the right mentors to help them grow and succeed. We believe in the power of personalized education and strive to bridge the gap between learners and instructors through a seamless, technology-driven platform. At SkillMatch, we are committed to innovation, accessibility, and continuous learning. Join us on this journey to make skill development easier and more effective for everyone!
